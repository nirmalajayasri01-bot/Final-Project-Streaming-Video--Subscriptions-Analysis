The primary objective of this project is to analyze MavenFlix subscription data from September 2022 to September 2023 to gain a comprehensive understanding of customer subscription patterns, retention behavior, and overall business performance. The analysis aims to identify trends in subscriber acquisition and cancellations over time, helping evaluate the platform's growth trajectory.

This project seeks to measure customer loyalty by examining subscription duration and determining the proportion of customers who remain subscribed for five months or longer. Understanding subscriber longevity can provide valuable insights into customer satisfaction and the effectiveness of retention efforts.

Another key objective is to calculate and compare monthly retention rates to identify the periods with the strongest and weakest customer retention. By highlighting months with unusually high churn or strong subscriber loyalty, MavenFlix can better understand seasonal trends and customer behavior.

The project also aims to evaluate the impact of subscription intervals, payment status, and cancellation patterns on customer retention. These insights can help uncover factors that contribute to long-term customer engagement or early subscription termination.

Furthermore, the analysis will support data-driven decision-making by identifying opportunities to improve customer retention, reduce churn, optimize subscription offerings, and increase recurring revenue. The findings can assist stakeholders in developing targeted marketing campaigns, customer engagement initiatives, and subscription strategies that enhance overall business performance.

Ultimately, the project intends to transform raw subscription data into actionable business insights that enable MavenFlix to strengthen customer relationships, improve subscriber retention, and sustain long-term growth in the competitive video streaming industry.
